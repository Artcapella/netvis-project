EVALUATION TASK QUESTIONS
Network Congestion & Uncertainty Visualization

INSTRUCTIONS:
Each scenario shows a time window of network link telemetry.
Condition A: Congestion-only view (no confidence information)
Condition B: Confidence-aware view (with uncertainty encoding)
Answer each question for both conditions.

--------------------------------------------------
SCENARIO 1: Clean Congestion Spike
Link: ATLAng__HSTNng, Time window: 950-1049

Q1.1: Is this link congested during this time window? (Yes/No)
Q1.2: At what timestep does peak congestion occur?
Q1.3: How confident are you in your answer to Q1.1? (1-5 scale)
Q1.4: [Condition B only] Does the confidence information change
       your interpretation? (Yes/No, explain)

--------------------------------------------------
SCENARIO 2: Noisy Hotspot
